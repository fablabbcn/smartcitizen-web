EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES TO UNDERSTAND THEIR ENVIRONMENT

We are a collective of passionate people who believe that environmental data is critical to inform civic participation. By developing free and open-source tools for environmental monitoring and methodologies for community-driven action, we empower citizen participation to collectively shape our cities.

BUILDING ON OUR HISTORY

The Smart Citizen project provides tools for collecting, understanding, and sharing environmental data. Since 2012, we have developed various iterations of the Smart Citizen Kit, allowing anyone to contribute and take part in data-driven action. Our newest Kit is now available through Seeed Studio.
